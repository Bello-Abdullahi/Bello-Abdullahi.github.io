I am a PhD Economics Student at [Lancaster University](https://www.lancaster.ac.uk). I am supervised by [Prof. Lorenza Rossi](https://sites.google.com/view/lorenza-rossi/home-page) and [Dr. Stefano Fasani](https://sites.google.com/view/stefano-fasani). I obtained my MSc in Applied Economics from the University of Strathclyde. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant (Economist) at the [Centre for the Study of the Economies of Africa (CSEA)](https://www.cseaafrica.org/), where I contributed to impactful studies and projects that inform development policies across Africa.

I am an EDI representative for the [North West Social Science Doctoral Training Partnership](https://nwssdtp.ac.uk/current-students/student-representatives/). Feel free to reach out if you have any concerns.

During my free time, I mentor graduate students from Africa who are interested in graduate opportunities. If you are from Africa, considering graduate school, and looking for someone to speak to—whether you are unsure of your decision, require guidance, or want someone to review your documents and provide feedback—shoot me an email [here](mailto:b.abdullahi@lancaster.ac.uk).

Under the [Northern Elite Scholars](https://nesplatform.com) platform, a Northern Nigeria-focused mentorship program, we have mentored and provided support to mentees in their graduate school applications. Over the years, this has resulted in over $1,000,000 in scholarship funding. If you are looking at supporting our initiative, please reach out.

You can contact me via email at [b.abdullahi@lancaster.ac.uk](mailto:b.abdullahi@lancaster.ac.uk). You can also find me on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/Muhammad_Bell0), [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=FONaAoAAAAAJ&hl=en), and [ORCID](https://orcid.org/0000-0001-5075-1812).
