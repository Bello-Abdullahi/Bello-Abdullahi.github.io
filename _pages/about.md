I am **Bello Abdullahi**, a PhD Economics student at [Lancaster University](https://www.lancaster.ac.uk). I am supervised by [Prof. Lorenza Rossi](https://sites.google.com/view/lorenza-rossi/home-page) and [Dr. Stefano Fasani](https://sites.google.com/view/stefano-fasani).

I hold an MSc in Applied Economics from the University of Strathclyde. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant (Economist) at the [Centre for the Study of the Economies of Africa (CSEA)](https://www.cseaafrica.org/), contributing to policy-focused development research.

I also serve as an EDI representative for the [North West Social Science Doctoral Training Partnership](https://nwssdtp.ac.uk/current-students/student-representatives/). In parallel, I mentor African students interested in graduate school opportunities through [Northern Elite Scholars](https://nesplatform.com).

You can reach me at [b.abdullahi@lancaster.ac.uk](mailto:b.abdullahi@lancaster.ac.uk).
